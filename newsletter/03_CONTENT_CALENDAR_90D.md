# Catalyst Edge — 90-Day Content Calendar (Prompt 03 output)

Positioning it ties to: "audited win rate on same-session catalyst plays."

## Send Frequency (sustainable, not impressive)

- **Daily brief** — Mon–Fri 7:30 AM ET. Auto-generated by `build_newsletter_picks.py`. Zero manual writing required per day. Sustainability = fully automated.
- **Weekly deep-dive** — Saturday 10 AM ET. One longform issue per week, human-written in under 90 minutes. 12 of these are the "12-issue arc" below.
- **No Sundays** unless halt/resume warrants `[ALERT]`.

Daily automated briefs + one weekly human issue = sustainable for an autonomous operator with finite attention. Not 7-per-week heroics that collapse by week 5.

## Content Type Mix (never 12 teaches in a row)

- Teach: 5 (W2, W4, W5, W7, W9)
- Story: 1 (W3)
- Perspective: 2 (W1, W10)
- Curate: 1 (W8)
- Challenge: 3 (W6, W11, W12)

Mix enforced: no two consecutive teach-type issues.

## Arc Logic (each issue reinforces positioning, no standalone filler)

- **Weeks 1–3** establish *why* Catalyst Edge exists (industry lies → filter → concrete example).
- **Weeks 4–5** add *defensive* and *offensive* signal layers (dilution + sympathy) — widens subscriber value beyond "picks."
- **Week 6** *proves* the differentiator publicly (scorecard with losses shown).
- **Weeks 7–9** deepen *domain authority* in sub-verticals readers self-select into.
- **Weeks 10–11** shift to *contrarian insight + community* — late-stage retention levers.
- **Week 12** *closes the loop and re-sells* — scorecard + model v2 teaser → upgrades + referrals.

Each weekly issue ends with a CTA tied to the next week, building anticipation instead of a static "see you next week."
