FAQ

The questions every founder, analyst, and PM asks before pulling the trigger.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Stripe handles billing. One-click cancel from the receipt email. No annual lock-in, no calls, no retention scripts. If you cancel mid-cycle you keep access until the period ends.

What data sources do you use?

SEC EDGAR XBRL companyfacts (US fundamentals + DCF), Yahoo Finance for international fundamentals + news, Stooq for OHLC, FRED + NOAA + USPTO + PACER + ThetaData for the broader data universe. Every DCF input traces to a public source.

Refund policy?

14-day no-questions refund on Reader and Pro. Just email opensource@example.com. Team and Enterprise refunds prorated by seat-month.

Multi-seat discount?

Team plan is $199/mo for up to 10 seats — that's already $19.90/seat vs $39 individual. Beyond 10, Enterprise pricing kicks in at $499+/seat/mo with volume discounts at 25/50/100 seats.

Can I white-label this for my fund?

Enterprise tier includes white-label dashboards and custom domain. Your LP-facing reports show your fund's brand, not ours. Talk to the founder.

How long do you retain my data?

Authentication and billing: indefinitely while account is active, deleted 90 days after cancellation. Watchlists/portfolios: stored only on your device unless you explicitly opt into Team sharing. We don't sell data, ever. SOC2-ready audit logs available on Enterprise.
