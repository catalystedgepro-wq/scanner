Who Should Use What

Use Catalyst Edge if:

You trade SEC filing catalysts (8-K, Form 4, S-3) and want scored, pre-market picks with squeeze signals, dark pool data, and insider clustering — all in one feed. Best for gap traders, momentum traders, and quants who want structured data before 4 AM ET.

Use Finviz if:

You need quick fundamental and technical screening. Great for finding stocks by P/E, market cap, or technical patterns. Doesn't analyze SEC filings or score catalysts.

Use TradingView if:

You need best-in-class charting with Pine Script backtesting. Excellent for technical analysis. No SEC filing parsing or catalyst scoring built in.

Use Benzinga Pro if:

You want real-time news and press releases with a squawk box. Strong for intraday news-driven trading. SEC filing coverage is news-headline based, not structured data.

They complement each other. Many traders use Catalyst Edge for pre-market catalyst identification, then TradingView for charting entries, and Finviz for quick fundamental checks. The tools aren't mutually exclusive.
