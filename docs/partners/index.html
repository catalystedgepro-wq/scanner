FAQ

Five quick answers to the questions every partner asks before signing up.

How long is the cookie window?

30 days. If a visitor clicks your ?ref= URL and converts within 30 days on any device that hits the same browser session, they're attributed to you. We use first-touch attribution — the first ref wins, never re-attributed.

Payout method + frequency?

Net 30 from the close of each calendar month. We support PayPal, ACH (US bank), and Wise for international. Minimum payout is $20; balances under $20 roll forward.

What are the attribution rules?

Honest disclosure required: don't disguise affiliate links as editorial endorsements. Don't run paid search bidding on "catalyst edge" branded terms. Don't impersonate the founder or the brand. Violations forfeit current and future commissions.

When do commissions stop?

The 6-month payout window starts from the customer's first paid charge, not their signup. If they cancel before month 6, you get paid through the cancellation date. After month 6 you'll have referred a new customer for us — that's the deal.

What about multi-product attribution?

If your referred customer upgrades from Reader → Pro → Team, you earn the share at each tier they sit in, prorated by tier-month. Downgrade → reduced commission. Cancellation → commission stops.

Can I run all three programs?

Yes. Affiliate + Reseller + Embed are independent programs and stack cleanly. A single referral can only attribute to one program though — first-touch wins. Most partners start as Affiliate then graduate to Embed once they have product surface.
