Integration notes

Three small things to know before shipping. Most embeds are a single iframe — no JS bundle, no auth flow.

🔌

Zero-build install

Drop the iframe anywhere — Webflow, Notion, your React/Vue/Svelte app, raw HTML. No script tag, no npm install, no CORS work. The iframe is fully self-contained.

🏷️

Partner attribution

Pass ?partner=YOUR_ID in the iframe URL. Every outbound click on the widget badge is tagged with your ID for the affiliate program — see /partners for details.

⚡

Performance budget

Widgets are < 8 KB gzipped HTML+CSS+JS. Lazy-loaded fonts, no third-party deps, no tracking pixels. They render in < 100ms on a cold visitor and refresh every 5 minutes thereafter.
