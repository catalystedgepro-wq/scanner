How Sympathy Chains Work

A sympathy play happens when a stock moves on a catalyst (8-K, earnings, FDA decision) and peers in the same sector or industry group move in the same direction — often within 1-2 trading days.

We track the trigger ticker, identify its sector peers, and measure the average peer move within T+1 day. High gap scores with multiple peers indicate the strongest sympathy chain setups.
