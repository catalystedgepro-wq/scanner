What Is Smart Money Tracking?

Institutional investors managing over $100M must disclose their holdings quarterly via SEC Form 13F. When multiple independent funds simultaneously increase positions in the same stock, it signals institutional consensus.

We aggregate 13F filings from EDGAR and rank stocks by the number of distinct funds holding or increasing positions. Higher fund counts with recent filing dates indicate fresh institutional conviction.
