- HUD: read-only demo mode (static snapshot)
- **Purpose:** Build audience. Morning Brew proved free content at scale beats paywalls for top-of-funnel.

### Edge Reader — $12/mo or $99/yr
- Full scanner: real-time data, all tickers, custom filters

- Newsletter archive + historical backtests

### Edge Pro — $39/mo or $349/yr
- Everything in Reader
- Full 3D HUD (Cerebro) with live data + AI co-pilot unlimited

- **This is the hero tier.** The HUD is the wow factor nobody else has.

### Edge API — $99/mo or $899/yr
- Everything in Pro
- REST API: catalyst scores, filing classifications, momentum signals
- Webhook alerts for custom triggers
- Bulk CSV export of historical catalyst data
- Commercial use license
- **Target:** quant traders, RIA firms, fintech devs. Even 50 subs = $60K/yr.

### Tip Jar — $0+ (bridge revenue)
- Ko-fi or Buy Me a Coffee on scanner, newsletter, explainer page
- Expect $200-800/mo from loyal free audience of 5,000+
- Zero effort, immediate revenue. Do it today.
