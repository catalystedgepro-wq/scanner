What is a Convergence Alert?

A convergence alert fires when multiple independent signals point to the same stock at the same time. Each signal alone is noise. When 3+ fire together, it becomes a high-conviction setup.

Squeeze — High short interest + volume surge
Insider — Form 4 cluster buys
Dark Pool — Large block volume
Smart Money — Institutional signals
SEC Filing — 8-K, S-3, Form 4 catalysts
Deep Value — Fundamentally undervalued

Our convergence engine cross-references 7 independent data streams and assigns a conviction score from 0-100. Scores above 40 are ELEVATED. Above 50 is HIGH conviction.
