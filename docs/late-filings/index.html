Why Late Filings Matter

An NT (Notification of Late Filing) is required when a company can't meet its SEC deadline. Common reasons: accounting restatements, auditor changes, internal control weaknesses, or pending material events.

Historically, NT filers underperform the market in the following 90 days. But when insiders are simultaneously buying, it can signal the delay is procedural rather than substantive — creating a contrarian setup.
