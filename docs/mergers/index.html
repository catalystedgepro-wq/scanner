What Are Merger Signals?

When a company files an SC TO-T (tender offer) or DEFA14A (proxy solicitation), it signals active acquisition negotiations. These filings are legally required and appear on EDGAR before the market fully prices the event.

Tender offers often come with a premium to current price. Proxy fights indicate activist investors pushing for board changes. Both create short-term catalysts that momentum traders watch closely.
